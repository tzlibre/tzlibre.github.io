Why TzLibre Tezos is a great idea with a huge disruptive potential. As per its immutable charter, Tezos Stiftung is obliged by Swiss law to fund the correct implementation of this idea. We believe DLS-Tezos is the wrong way to implement it.

What is TzLibre TzLibre is a trustless and hard-capped implementation of Tezos. It is based on a fork of DLS-Tezos: it shares with it the underlying technology. The TzLibre blockchain is alternative to, and not compatible with, the DLS-Tezos blockchain.
