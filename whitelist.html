Next steps

When splitting begins, we'll issue a pre-launch TZL token on the Ethereum blockchain. Only whitelisted addresses will be allowed to claim TZL tokens. Following the EOS example, pre-launch tokens will then be converted into native coins when TzLibre mainnet launches.
