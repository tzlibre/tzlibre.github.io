Whitelist your Tezos ICO address

Who

Any Tezos ICO contributor can participate. Only Tezos ICO addresses can be whitelisted.

When

The whitelist is currently open. Our community is still discussing whether to close the whitelist in the coming days (to reward early adopters), or to close it later (to accomodate late adopters). We'll announce on our Telegram channel when the whitelist has been closed.

Need help?

If you're having trouble adding your Tezos public key hash (a/k/a Tezos address) to the whitelist, please doublecheck your contribution PDF and try again. Please use the latest version of the Firefox browser, preferably in private mode. If you still can't whitelist your address, you can contact us.
