Link your Ethereum address

After delegating TzLibre you must link your Tezos address with an Ethereum address. To do so you must prove ownership of your Tezos address by signing your current Ethereum address with your Tezos private key. You won't be able to change your Ethereum address afterwards. We suggest to use a brand new Ethereum address. Your signature will be public. To produce your signature download our web tool, then open it and follow the instructions.
