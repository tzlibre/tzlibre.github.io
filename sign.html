Verify your Tezos address
You can confirm your Ethereum address. To do so you must prove ownership of your Tezos address by signing your current Ethereum address with your Tezos private key. You won't be able to change your Ethereum address afterwards. We suggest to use a brand new Ethereum address. Your signature will be public. You can produce your signature with our web tool: download, open and follow the instructions. Alternatively, if your KYC-Tezos wallet allows you to sign messages, you can do so by signing your Ethereum address string.
