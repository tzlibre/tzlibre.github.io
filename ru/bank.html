Bond Bank

What

Bond Bank is a more efficient way to delegate TzLibre and earn TZL.

Who

Any XTZ holder can deposit in the Bond Bank and thus earn TZL.

Why

Delegating via the Bond Bank is more profitable than standard delegation. The Bond Bank helps TzLibre scale its baking operations, which is key to override KYC on KYC-Tezos and to raise seed funding to bootstrap TzLibre.

How

TzLibre Bond Bank is a Liquidity smart contract deployed on the KYC-Tezos network at the address KT1V7VoyjbvqSmnRtv9pHkRuBCPT7UubCrCX. It allows XTZ holders to deposit and withdraw XTZ at any time.

Current rewards

  • Current reward: 5%/month
  • Current TZL price: 0.033 XTZ
  • Reward currency: TZL
  • Payout schedule: monthly
  • Example reward: Depositing 1000 XTZ will generate 1500 TZL / month
  • Rewards are paid out to the linked Ethereum address

Terms

  • Minimum deposit (per account): 1000 XTZ
  • Maximum deposit (per account): n/a
  • Maximum deposits (global): 300,000 XTZ
  • Withdrawal fee: 0.1%
  • Minimum collateralization: 25%

RoI

At 5%/month depositing in the Bond Bank is:

  • 4.4x more profitable than delegating TzLibre with a standard delegation
  • 5.6x more profitable than the best KYC-Tezos delegate (5% fee)

How it works

The smart contract holds a minimum of 25% of deposited XTZ locked as collateral. It will therefore allow TzLibre to withdraw up to 75% to use XTZ as baking bonds. Depositrs can withdraw their own deposit at any given time. When a depositor withdraws TzLibre subsequently withdraws a corresponding part of its baking bond in order to recollateralize the smart contract.

Wallet setup

To successfully interact with the Bond Bank you must use a wallet that enables interaction with smart contracts. There are currently only 2 wallets that allow interaction with smart contracts.

  • Advanced users: offical tezos-client
  • Normal users: LibreBox

How to deposit

  1. Check you are using a smart contract enabled wallet (see above). If the wallet is not funded yet, move funds from your KT1 (e.g. the address you're currently delegating TzLibre with) or tz1 address in order to fund it.
  2. Deposit your funds: send your funds to the smart contract address KT1V7VoyjbvqSmnRtv9pHkRuBCPT7UubCrCX
  3. Check your deposit was successful: your wallet balance will decrease by the deposited amount and you'll find the deposit in your transaction history

How to withdraw

  1. Send 1 XTZ to the smart contract address KT1V7VoyjbvqSmnRtv9pHkRuBCPT7UubCrCX
  2. Check your withdrawal was successful: your wallet balance will grow by the withdrawn amount and you'll find the withdrawal in your transaction history

More info

The purpose of the Bond Bank is to allow for trustless lending of XTZ to increase TzLibre baking bond. By capping the borrowable amount and by spreading the remaining counterparty risk among multiple depositors, this smart contract allows users to withdraw their own deposit at any time. TzLibre will borrow XTZ from the contract in order to increase its baking bonds. Larger baking bonds allow TzLibre to accept an ever growing number of delegations.
