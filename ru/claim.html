Claim your TZL tokens

Who

Contributors who already whitelisted their Tezos address can claim TZL by linking it with an Ethereum address under their own control.

When

The whitelist is currently open. Our community is still discussing whether to close the whitelist in the coming days (to reward early adopters), or to close it later (to accomodate late adopters). We'll announce on our Telegram channel when the whitelist has been closed.

Need help?

If you're having trouble adding your Tezos public key hash (a/k/a Tezos address) to the whitelist, please doublecheck your contribution PDF and try again. Please use the latest version of the Firefox browser, preferably in private mode. If you still can't whitelist your address, you can contact us.

As a contributor to Tezos Stiftung (CHE-290.597.458) I recognize, welcome and support the existence of multiple implementations of the Tezos idea.

I expect Tezos Stiftung to act in the best interest of contributors by fostering competition among these implementations, and to do so by directly funding research and development of interoperability solutions that enable the seamless transfer of value and data among various Tezos blockchains.

I request that any third-party product developed with Tezos Stiftung funding shall always be compatible with each and every major Tezos blockchain.
