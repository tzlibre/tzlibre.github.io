Delegate TzLibre

What

To receive TZL airdrops you must delegate TzLibre. You can revoke, cancel or change your delegation at any time. If you delegate TzLibre you will receive TZL airdrops as reward.

Who

Any XTZ address can delegate TzLibre and thus earn TZL.

Info

To learn more about TzLibre delegation read our delegation FAQ. For rewards and timeline of the current airdrop check our subreddit. To learn more about how KYC-Tezos delegation works read this guide.

How

  1. Activate your XTZ allocation on the genesis block with Activatez.
  2. Create a TezBox wallet with your fundraiser data: visit TezBox, select 'Restore Tezbox' then 'Fundraiser Wallet'.
  3. Insert your data: PDF seed words, PDF email address, ICO password, and KYC activation code.
  4. Create a new KT1 account (a special account for delegation) by clicking the 'Add Account' button (fee: 0.25 XTZ)
  5. Move all your XTZ from your tz1 account to your KT1 account: from the main wallet select 'Send', enter your KT1 address in the 'To' address, enter all your XTZ, and click 'Send' (leave fee field blank).
  6. In the KT1 account, select the 'Delegate' menu and then choose 'Custom delegate'.
  7. Insert address tz1iDu3tHhf7H4jyXk6rGV4FNUsMqQmRkwLp and confirm.
  8. 24 hours after you delegated, check your airdrops status on our verification page and check your delegation status on this file (.CSV).

    You can also delegate TzLibre using the official KYC-Tezos fullnode as well as other wallets (see this list of KYC-Tezos wallets). TzLibre delegate address is tz1iDu3tHhf7H4jyXk6rGV4FNUsMqQmRkwLp
