ERC-20 Migration

How to

  1. Install Metamask
  2. Generate a brand new Ethereum address
  3. Generate a brand new TzLibre address with LibreBox (without using hardware wallets)
  4. Choose how many TZL ERC-20 to migrate
  5. Use the brand new TzLibre address generated at step 3 as the destination address
  6. Click on submit button (few ETH required to pay fee)

Confirmation notice

Your TZL ERC-20 tokens are now burned: you will receive the same amount of native TZL coins in the TzLibre network in 1-5 working days.

Migrate your ERC-20 TZL pre-launch tokens into native TZL coins.
